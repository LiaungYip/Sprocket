Dense Ores

NOTE: Dense ores have a 10% chance of replacing their ore of choice.
      They do not spawn of their own volition.  This oregen will likely
      need a way to block vanilla Minecraft ores from spawning, or wipe
      them out after they are placed.
      
      Dense Ores are best set as a duplicate of the original vanilla
      ores, except instead of being just "minecraft:something_ore", they
      will instead be "minecraft:something_ore, denseores:block0:?" with
      respective Block Weights values of "0.9" and "0.1."
